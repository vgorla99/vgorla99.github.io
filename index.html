About

Unusual path.
Focused execution.

I built and ran a crypto and AI community from 2022 to 2024 — learning how online audiences work, how to manage communities at scale, and where technology was heading.

In 2023 I co-founded MindShift, a startup that shipped an AI chatbot on WhatsApp for influencers. That was my first real taste of product-market fit, technical debt, and everything in between.

In 2024 I earned my BJJ Black Belt — something that took years of discipline and showing up when it wasn't easy. That same year I went all-in on building: apps, automation systems, and AI solutions. Based in Nuremberg, Germany. Open to remote.
